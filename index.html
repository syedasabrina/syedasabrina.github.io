Syeda Sabrina Akter


Hello! I'm Syeda Sabrina Akter and I'm a third-year Computer Science PhD student at George Mason University. The primary focus is studying Computational Approaches of Multilingual News Framing Analysis. My current research focuses on leveraging LLMs and crowdsourcing techniques to scale up the Framing Analysis Task. I also have experience in working with Speech Recognition and Translation tasks. I'm lucky to be advised by Prof. Antonis Anastasopoulos and be a part of GMU's NLP lab .

Before starting my PhD at Mason, I worked as a Lecturer in Northern University Bangladesh for two years. I am also currently working as a Teaching Assistant for the department of Computer Science. I received my BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Professionals in 2018. My bachelor's thesis focused on developing a machine learning framework for sorting and classifying unstructured data from resumes.

In my free time I like to read, and travel. One of my bucket list task is to visit all 50 states of the USA within the next few years.

I have already visited 13 states. (And, DC. Does it count??) (Last updated: July 2023)
